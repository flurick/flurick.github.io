Reasons of bad open software

Some sort of summary of Vannevar symposium 1995.

If you sell closed sourced programs, only you can fix it. If you make open sourced progamms, you trust the user to fix it. if you do not trust your user to work on it, you might grow to dislike them to the point of not fixing it yourself. It takes some will power to work on your own projects, and even the slightest possibility of you not having to do it your self reduces the possisbility of you getting anything done, since you might not have to.

Getting into the mindset that it is all on you might work for a while, until you have given your everything expecting something in return. Surprise! It's a process not a project, there is no end unless you say so, but you never would, since you are a good person and heroes do not leave things half done, right?

Everything is wrong

Make a computer for children, since they both will be the normla user when they have grown up, and also, kids are very eager to learn how they fit in the world. Do they need something simple and naive like overlapping windows, maybe not.

It's easy to get angry at what we did not do, but not helpful. If there is a better place to be we should guide ourself there, the ones already there might even keep a lookout for where to go next, and help the rest of us to avoid falling into the same pits that they already have explored closer then they ever wanted to.

Everything is OK

That guy that was way better then you in school in that one subject is not working with that today, it did not matter that he was once good at it compared the his peers. That one idea that could reduce stress globaly is not understod by more then a few. Thecnology exist that can save time, pain and maybe even life, but is not actually used. That is not how our world works. If the perfect creature wandered our planet, it would probably get squased by an elefant.
